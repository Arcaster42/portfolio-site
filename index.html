Background


I received my first computer in 1993 at the age of five. My first experience involved launching a shareware copy of Doom by accessing the command prompt. Since 2000, I have been programming as a hobby starting with Visual Basic, mostly playing with WinForms and fumbling my way through some concepts such as classes and methods. Eventually I got heavily into gaming and, over time, became very familiar with PC hardware. Throughout gaming, I developed programming fundamentals using in-game editors, modding, and development engines.

At 19 I established myself in the workforce as management in a national electronics retail company where I developed a greater knowledge of hardware. It was during this time that I began teaching myself German, studying daily. Over the next several years, I vacationed to Germany several times while working and moving into a commercial construction management position.

I decided I wanted to run my own company and obtain a college degree, so at 23, I enrolled in Kennesaw State University and incorporated Eonian Enterprises, LLC. This company served as a management and IT consulting company that gave me opportunities to work with several small companies, evolving into a small business development firm over time. During this time, I used contacts in Germany to secure an internship at Thyssen Schachtbau, an international mining and engineering firm. Having felt the need to make up for lost time, I overloaded my semesters and graduated in only three years.

After graduating, I received an offer to work in Hiroshima, Japan. Having been eager to be abroad, I accepted the position and was moved within two weeks. Arriving without knowing any Japanese, I began self-studying until I became conversational. It was during this time that I had the greatest development in my programming skills in C# and R while developing a strong interest in statistics and data science.

Now at 30, I am seeking an opportunity to settle into a permanent, fulfilling career that allows me to use my current and developing skillsets while continuing to improve my expertise and add new abilities. Having such a broad range of experiences, I have become incredibly well-rounded, but my ultimate goal now is to achieve a great level of depth, and I intend to do so by using my greatest ability: desire and capacity to learn.
